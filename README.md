# Superstore Sales EDA

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Sample Superstore dataset using Python. The objective is to identify sales trends, profitability drivers, regional performance, and the relationship between discounts and profit to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing and underperforming categories
- Compare regional sales
- Study the impact of discounts on profit
- Detect outliers in sales
- Visualize business insights using charts

---

# Key Findings

- Technology generated the highest total sales.
- Technology also recorded the highest average sales per transaction.
- Furniture generated moderate sales but comparatively lower profitability.
- Office Supplies contributed the lowest total sales among the three categories.
- High discounts frequently resulted in lower or negative profits.
- Sales contained several extreme outliers, indicating a few very high-value orders.
- Sales varied across regions, highlighting regional performance differences.
- Monthly sales exhibited seasonal trends with noticeable peaks.
